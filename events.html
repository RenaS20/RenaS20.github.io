DS Discovery Symposium

The Data Discovery Symposium features poster presentations from student researchers. Working with mentors across campus and industry, Data Discovery participants use their data skills to design analyses, build tools, and present solutions to real-world challenges. I presented my CourseWise Education Meta-Analytics II project, showcasing an internal metrics dashboard built to track platform engagement and institutional adoption.

Amazon x CS Kickstart — Emotional Intelligence Can Supercharge Your Career

From navigating rejections and interviews to building emotional competence at work, we explored how EQ is the often-overlooked skill that carries us through some of the most defining career moments. This event, organized through CS Kickstart's industry partnership with Amazon, brought together students and professionals to discuss the human side of building a career in tech.
